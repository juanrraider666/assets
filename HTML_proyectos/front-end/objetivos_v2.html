Purpose: To represent the performance of a goal in a percentage. The goal reached and how far away from the goal or over the goal will be visualized.
Requirements: A fixed or variable goal by individual will be needed.

  • Sales Related - Track the goal of: sales revenue and units; the number of deals closed and opened; the number of new clients recruited

Goal Tracker with Pace
Graphic Type: Number with a binary indicator representing above or below pace.
Purpose: To show a result in a number or percentage form and indicate if an individual’s performance is above or below the average or threshold.
Requirements: A goal is needed; decide the use of a group average or a threshold.
Examples:
  • Identify if an individual is above or below the average revenue
  • Identify if an individual is above or below pace for revenue; units; deals closed; customer served; chats; emails; calls; new clients recruited; and gym trips.
