Hotel Reservation
Fortinet negotiated a special All-Inclusive rate for the Partners attending the XTREME & Partner Advisory Board at the GRAND at Moon Palace Resort Cancun, Mexico. Attendees will need to book their hotel stay in order to complete their event registration.

To maintain the group rate your reservation must be completed by July 31, 2019 thru the Registration form. Attendees will be charged the full amount of total stay at time of booking.

Rates (Meals, taxes and tips included):
  • $295 USD per person per night – Single room (1 adult)
  • $195 USD per person per night – Double room (2 adults)
Rates Include:
  • All meals and drinks at the Hotel
  • 24-hour room service
  • Unlimited free phone calls to USA, Canada and landlines in Mexico
  • All Hotel taxes and gratuities
  • Wireless Internet
  • Check in time at 3:00 PM local time
  • Check Out is at 12:00 PM local time
IMPORTANT: If the hotel reservation is made through any other source, the rate will vary. Registrations done after July 31 will be upon room availability and hotel rate at that time, this could be up to $450.00 USD (per person, per night – single room)

Cancellation Policies Cancellations must be received at least one week prior to arrival or entire stay will be charged.

About Cancun, Mexico
With 14 miles of magnificent white sand beaches and turquoise blue waters from the Caribbean Sea, Cancun is the ultimate vacation destination. With an array of world-class luxury hotels along its coast, it is no surprise Cancun welcomes an average of 10 Million tourists every year. This vibrant city, located in the Quintana Roo region of Mexico, offers endless possibilities of adventure - from daring water sports to an exuberant nightlife scene, and everything in between.
