Arrival Instructions:

International: Upon your arrival to the Cancun International Airport you will be guided to the immigration reception area where you will show all the required entry documents such as passports, visas, tourist cards, etc. Entry documents will be provided by your airline and will be available at the reception area. If you are not sure if you need special papers to enter the country, please read the Immigration tab or contact your nearest Mexican Consulate prior to your arrival to Cancun.

The transportation company will be waiting for you outside of your arrival terminal after you exit customs, with the Fortinet XTREME logo Signage.

The transportation company will be tracking your flight in case it is delayed or arriving early. Only if your flight is cancelled or you were switched to another flight, please make sure the transportation company gets the new flight information so they can re-schedule your transfer.

Domestic: All domestic flights arrive on Terminal 2. Upon your arrival, proceed to the baggage claim area to claim your luggage and continue to exit the terminal. If you are on a connecting flight, please ask an airport representative for your next flight. You may need to be transferred to a different terminal.

The transportation company will be waiting for you outside of your arrival terminal after you exit customs, with the Fortinet XTREME logo Signage.

Departure Instructions:
At your arrival please confirm or update your departure information at the Event Information Desk at the Groups Lobby, Grand Foyer.

About Cancun, Mexico
With 14 miles of magnificent white sand beaches and turquoise blue waters from the Caribbean Sea, Cancun is the ultimate vacation destination. With an array of world-class luxury hotels along its coast, it is no surprise Cancun welcomes an average of 10 Million tourists every year. This vibrant city, located in the Quintana Roo region of Mexico, offers endless possibilities of adventure - from daring water sports to an exuberant nightlife scene, and everything in between.
