Have Questions? We Have Answers
q. What is the difference between the XTREME & the Partner Advisory Board?
a. XTREME 2019 is a four-day cybersecurity enablement event that includes keynote sessions and exclusive hands-on training created to increase the knowledge of Fortinet Latin America and Caribbean top partners while empowering them to become cybersecurity experts. The XTREME is an exclusive, invitation-only, VIP event and attending is a privilege for every engineer. Only engineers who receive the XTREME invitation can attend the training and participate in the event.

The Partner Advisory Board First Edition is a two-day forum that includes keynote sessions and executive networking, exclusively for chosen partners from Latin America and the Caribbean. Members will have the opportunity to discuss Fortinet’s strategy firsthand with leaders while experiencing XTREME 2019, the best cybersecurity training in the industry. Only members who receive the exclusive invitation can join the board and participate in the event. Invitation cannot be transferable.
q. Do I need to confirm my attendance?
a. Yes. If you have been invited, it doesn’t mean you are confirmed. Please confirm your attendance by completing your Registration before July 15th, 2019
q. Is there a specific hotel where I can get a special rate?
a. Fortinet has negotiated a special rate for their partners at the GRAND at Moon Palace Resort. It is suggested to choose this hotel, which will offer a special All-Inclusive rate to XTREME attendees. If you want to stay at another hotel please consider the extra cost for the Day Pass to access the event hotel which is USD $221 per person per day plus the transportation costs and times, which may vary due to traffic.
q. What is the rate at the GRAND at Moon Palace Resort?
a. Fortinet has negotiated a special All-Inclusive rate at the GRAND at Moon Palace Resort for the XTREME attendees of USD $295 per person per night for single occupancy and USD $195 per person per night for double occupancy.
q. Will Fortinet pay for hotel and/or meals expenses?
a. Partners have to pay for their hotel expenses. If you are staying at the GRAND at Moon Palace Resort, meals are included in the hotel rate. If you want to stay at another hotel please consider the extra cost for the Day Pass to access the event hotel which is USD $221 per person per day plus the transportation costs and times, which may vary due to traffic.
q. How many partners from my company can go to the training?
a. Fortinet understands the partners attending the XTREME will share knowledge back with their coworkers once they return to their office, on sessions that will better suit their internal schedule. Because of this, all invited Partners are scheduled to send 1 (one) engineer or sales executive to the training.

Companies wishing to send more than one engineer are welcomed to put the second and third engineers on waiting list. Should some of the invited partners not confirm before July 15 Fortinet will notify of available seats to companies with engineers on the waiting list.
q. What language will be used at the event?
a. The majority of the event will be developed mainly in Spanish, but some sessions will be held in English. Simultaneous translations will be provided from English to Portuguese and Spanish on Monday. No simultaneous translations to any language will be provided from Tuesday through Thursday

q. My laptop has less memory / processor / disk than requested, can I bring it anyway?
a. The XTREME this year will heavily use Virtual Machines. If you bring less hardware than requested, you risk not having enough resources to do the labs, or falling behind due slowness on your laptop.

It is strongly recommended to bring as much disk, memory and processor as you can on your laptop.
