Instructions to register your Access Points:
Start by entering the date, place, invoice number and serial numbers of the purchase you have made. You can only enter an invoice number that matches the information entered. Please note that the document you upload must be in the following formats, PDF, JPG or PNG with a max. 5 MB.
Please enter a valid info
The serial number field has a limit of 10 serial numbers per entry. If for some reason you cannot find the serial number in the list, enter your serial number manually in the "Enter serial number" field, this information will be validated as new serial numbers are updated.
Note: Any serial number that remains "pending" will be automatically removed from your list, after three months from the date it was loaded.
Keep in mind new serial numbers are uploaded weekly!
