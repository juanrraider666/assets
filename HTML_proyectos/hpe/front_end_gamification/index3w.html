Fantasy
This is the make-believe world of Super Heroes where your imagination can run as wild and powerful as you let it, while unleashing the powers you have in your work environment.
  • Start playing by activating your super Hero: There are two Super Heroes (male & female)
  • There are 5 main objectives and 29 mini objectives. Make sure you follow them up to earn accessories and powers to dress-up your Super Hero during the year.
  • Make sure you click on each of the objectives to know what you have to do to earn your super powers. Don’t forget that we are expecting you to win the 5 main powers a year… to be the exemplary HPE E&G Super Hero of the year.
  • Make sure you click on each of the mini objectives to know what you have to do to earn your super powers. Don’t forget that we are expecting you to win the 29 accessories during the year to measure your progress against the game objectives.
